At Raising Rapids we would love to have you, your family, and your friends to come and experience the wonders of the Grand Canyon. Northern Arizona has been blessed by over 250 miles worth of the Colorado River and it is one of the best places in the United States to challenge your whitewater rafting skills. While the rapids are at times difficult, like Lava Falls, Gore’s Canyon, and Tunnel Falls, the adrenaline rush and scenic views make each moment memorable and rewarding. While on your grand adventurer we want you to be aware of some other exciting possibilities listed below!

Experiences

The Grand Canyon is full of wildlife, scenic views, and chances to make new friends. While you are here keep an eye out for these few things!

Many animals call the Canyon home! Bighorn sheep, bald eagles, and even cougars can be seen on rare occasions.

Breathtaking Scenery: Redwall Cavern, Vasey’s Paradise, Elves Chasm, Deer Creek Falls. (AND MORE!)

Best of all the Canyon can bring you and your friends closer together with long lasting memories of your trip.
